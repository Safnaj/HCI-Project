Rooftop Ocean View Pool

On the 30th floor of this modern building facing the ocean, you find Rooftop infinity pool, and right next to it is a lovely rooftop pool. Called 'Plunge Pool', so you understand it is not the biggest (or deepest) rooftop pool around, but offering exclusive poolside lounging with amazing views over all of the Waikiki still makes it one of the top spots in the world. Enjoy handcrafted cocktails from the rooftop bar, which is open to all.

Rooftop Ocean View Jacuzzi

Enjoy our hot tub in the rooftop along with the mini bar snacks. We all know how relaxing it is to soak in a hot tub after a tough day. But did you know that hot tubs actually offer a variety of health benefits? From muscle relaxation and joint pain relief to improvements in blood pressure, there's never been a better excuse to hop in a Jacuzzi hot tub at the end of the day
