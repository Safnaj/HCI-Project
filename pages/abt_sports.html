Water Sports

Join our Sports Club to enjoy your vacation ,engaging in water sports , beach games , outdoor games for both kids and adults.

Located on the water in Waikiki Marina Oahu, just minutes from Honolulu, we offer a full line up of water activities. The center has it all - Parasailing, Jetsking, Wakeboarding, and Water-skiing. Don't forget to try the exciting Banana Boat and Bumper Tube rides. Being only minutes from Hanauma Bay a snorkeling trip to this wonder is a must. Check out our three ride special today!     Parasailing over the clear blue crystal waters of Maunalua Bay and view the magnificent coastline of Oahu from Diamond Head to Koko Head in Waikiki / Honolulu. A shuttle boat will leave our dock and transport you to the parasail boat where our experienced captains will fly you 300 ft over the clear Pacific Ocean. For added fun you can be dunked in the cool refreshing water. This amazing ride will leave you breathless and keep you coming back for more. All prices are per person. *Riders must weigh at least 60lbs in normal wind conditions. There will be a $2 fuel surcharge added per activity per person. Due to the nature of the water sports industry, our activities are subject to delays. We appreciate your patience and Aloha Spirit.
